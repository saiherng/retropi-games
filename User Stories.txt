User Stories
--------------

As a gamer, I would like to play classic console games on a computer so that I don't have to buy a new console.

As a traveller, I would like to enjoy classic mini games in hotel room TVs without having to carry a laptop.  

As an amaetur game developer, I would like to experience the functionalaties and environment of games from early classic consoles.  


Acceptance Criteria
------------------

I can download any classic games from the internet and load them into the gaming device. 

I dont want to carry a laptop. 

I can save my game play data and start from where I left off.
